Ingredients


CRUST

1 package (15-1/2 ounces) Oreo cookies, crushed, divided
1/4 cup butter, melted
1/2 gallon vanilla ice cream, softened
1/4 cup cashews, finely chopped

ICE CREAM

1 half gallon vanilla ice cream, softened

FUDGE LAYER

1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
1 jar (16 ounces) hot fudge ice cream topping, warmed
1 carton (8 ounces) frozen whipped topping, thawed

2 (5-ounce) cans evaporated milk
1 teaspoon vanilla









Preparations




1. In a large bowl, combine 3-3/4 cups cookie crumbs and butter. Press into a greased 13x9-in. dish. Spread with ice cream; cover and freeze until set.

2. Drizzle fudge topping over ice cream; cover and freeze until set. Spread with whipped topping; sprinkle with remaining cookie crumbs. Cover and freeze 2 hours or until firm. Remove from the freezer 10 minutes before serving.

Test Kitchen Tips

To make cutting a breeze, try dipping your knife in hot water.
Wipe the blade periodically to clean it.
Skip the whipped topping and use whipped cream in a can to pipe pretty decorations on top.
It's a great way to keep little ones busy.

Nutrition Facts

1 piece: 573 calories
27g fat (14g saturated fat)
49mg cholesterol, 353mg sodium
76g carbohydrate (46g sugars, 2g fiber)
6g protein.
